3. Description of Services

Case Helix scores incoming disability leads by analyzing claimant-provided information against SSA eligibility criteria, including medical evidence quality indicators, vocational grid profile factors, application stage, SSA eligibility fundamentals, and functional limitation data. Scored results, including a numeric score and recommended action tier, are returned to the Client for use in intake prioritization.

Scored leads flow into the Client's designated CRM or intake system via our integration layer. The specific features, scoring factors, and integration methods available to a Client are set forth in the order documentation or onboarding agreement applicable to that Client's account.
